Frequently Asked Questions

How do I invite the bot to my server?

Click the "Invite Bot" button above and follow the comprehensive authorization process. Make sure you have "Manage Server" permissions on your Discord server to enable all advanced features.

Is the bot free to use?

Yes! Our bot offers a comprehensive free tier with most features included. Premium features are available for advanced server management needs and enterprise-level functionality.

How do I configure the advanced moderation settings?

Use the /config command to access the advanced moderation panel. You can customize auto-mod rules, set punishment levels, configure AI-powered warning systems, and enable real-time threat detection.

What permissions does the bot require?

The bot requires basic permissions like Send Messages, Read Message History, and Manage Messages for core functionality. Advanced features may require specific permissions which are clearly outlined during the invitation process.

How can I get premium support?

Join our premium support server using the "Join Support" button above. Our expert team and vibrant community are ready to help with any questions, issues, or customization requests you may have.
